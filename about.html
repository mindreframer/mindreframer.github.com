about

Hi, I'm Roman and I am a software engineer

I don't ask for permission to think differently, that is my basic right

I fail quite a lot, but that is the only way to test boundaries

I love to tinker, try new ideas, experiment with things, break them apart, put them together

I love to ship things and I love to be around other 'shippers'

I fix things and sometimes I also break things...

I like moving fast, this overcomes inertia and generates energy

I help my team to deliver great maintainable products on time, fostering personal growth and culture of sharing

I contantly question my past experiences and re-evaluate it from new perspectives

I prefer concepts over concrete frameworks, simplicity over new trendy fads
